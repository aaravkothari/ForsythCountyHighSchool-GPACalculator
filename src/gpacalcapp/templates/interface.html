Introduction

Hello! I'm Aarav Kothari, a member of the Denmark FBLA High School Chapter. Welcome to the guide for utilizing my Denmark High School's GPA Calculator. This tool is designed to help danes effortlessly calculate your weighted and unweighted GPAs based on the courses you've taken. If you have any specific questions, make sure to explore the Interactive Help Menu accessible through the navigation.

Instructions

How to Input
  • Keyboard: Use the keyboard to assist in inputting your current gpas and credits and with inputting your course names and grades. You are only permitted to enter numbers in numerical input forms.
  • Dropdowns: Click the dropdowns to select the correct value that aligns with your class type and credits.
Current GPA and Credits

To start of, view your transcript in infinite campus(accessible through the navigation) to see if you have your current weighted and unweighted GPA. If you do:

  • Input your current weighted and unweighted GPAs in the respective fields.
  • You are strongly advised to input your Current Total Credits Hours which is also stated in your transcript. This will ensure accurate GPA calculations as you go on to add courses in the calculator below.
Courses

Inputting courses into the calculator will automatically calculate and display your Final GPA.

  • Name: Input the name of the course to stay organized.
  • Type: Every high school class is either a regular class (4.0), AP class (5.0), or Dual Enrollment class (5.0). Make sure to select the correct type.
  • Credits: Classes are usually 1 credit. However, make sure to change this value because high school class credits can sometimes range from 0.5 to 2.0
  • Grade: Input the grade of the respective course.
Adding Courses
  1. Click the "Add Course" button to add a new row for entering information about an additional course.
  2. Fill in the details for the new course in the generated row.
Deleting Courses
  1. Locate the "Delete" button next to the course you want to remove.
  2. Click the "Delete" button to remove this course.
Final GPA Display

You can view the final weighted and unweighted GPAs on the lefthand display area.

  • The calculated GPAs take into account the details of each course's type, credits, and grades.
